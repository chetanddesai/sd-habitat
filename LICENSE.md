## Content Attribution

### Plant & Wildlife Data

Plant inventory data, maintenance schedules, phenology timelines, and wildlife interaction data were manually curated from [Calscape](https://calscape.org/), [iNaturalist](https://www.inaturalist.org/), the [National Wildlife Federation](https://www.nwf.org/), and native plant care references.

### Species Images

Plant and wildlife photographs are sourced dynamically from [iNaturalist](https://www.inaturalist.org) community observations via the taxa API. Each image is provided under its own Creative Commons license as specified by the original photographer. Image attribution is displayed alongside each photo.

### Observation Data

Citizen-science observation counts are sourced from the [iNaturalist](https://www.inaturalist.org) observations API, scoped to the Poway, CA area. Plant observation data is fetched via a helper script; wildlife observation data is fetched client-side at runtime.
